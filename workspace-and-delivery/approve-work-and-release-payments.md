# Approve Work and Release Payments

**Who this is for:** Business / Client / Project Owner

## What you'll achieve

- Approve completed milestones with confidence
- Automatically release pre-funded escrow payments to Freelancers

## Prerequisites

- Active business account
- An active project with at least one milestone funded into escrow before work begins
- You are the project owner or have approval permissions
- Freelancers has submitted work or marked the milestone as ready for review

## Steps

- **Fund the milestone into escrow** Before the milestone starts, you pay the agreed amount into an escrow account. **Expected result:** Funds are securely held and ready for release.
- **Review submitted work** Open the project and review the milestone deliverables. **Expected result:** You can confirm the work meets agreed requirements.
- **Mark the milestone or work as *Completed*** Change the milestone status to **Completed** once you are satisfied. **Expected result:** Escrow funds are released automatically.
- **Automatic payment release from escrow** The pre-funded amount is instantly transferred to the Freelancers's wallet. **Expected result:** No manual payment step is required.
- **Confirmation and record** Approval and payment release appear in the project activity log. **Expected result:** Clear documentation for tracking and audits.

## FAQ

- **When do I pay for the milestone?** You fund the milestone before work begins. The money is held in escrow.
- **Do I need to pay again after approval?** No. Approval simply releases the escrowed funds.
- **What happens if I don't approve the work yet?** Funds remain in escrow until the milestone is completed, revised, or disputed.
- **Can I cancel a milestone after funding?** This depends on platform policies and the project status.
- **Is escrow safe?** Yes. Escrow protects both the business and the Freelancers.

## Troubleshooting

- **Cannot mark milestone as completed**
  *Cause:* Milestone is not funded.
  *Fix:* Fund the milestone before approving work.
- **Payment released by mistake**
  *Cause:* Milestone marked completed accidentally.
  *Fix:* Contact support immediately.
- **Freelancers reports payment not received**
  *Cause:* Wallet setup incomplete.
  *Fix:* Ask the Freelancers to complete wallet verification.
