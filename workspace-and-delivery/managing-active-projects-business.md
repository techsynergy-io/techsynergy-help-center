# Managing Your Active Projects on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Track project progress and milestone completion efficiently
- Ensure timely delivery while protecting payments and project quality

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Business account
- At least one active project or milestone funded in escrow
- Defined deliverables and deadlines for milestones

## Steps to Manage Your Active Projects

### 1. Monitor Milestone Progress

- Track each milestone's status and submission dates
- Check that Freelancers updates the platform with completed work
- Set reminders for deadlines to avoid delays

### 2. Review Deliverables

- Verify that each submission meets the agreed specifications
- Provide timely approval or feedback to the Freelancers
- Request revisions directly through TechSynergy if needed

### 3. Communicate Clearly

- Keep all communication on TechSynergy's messaging system
- Clarify requirements and answer Freelancers questions promptly
- Avoid moving project discussions off-platform

### 4. Manage Payments

- Ensure each milestone is funded before the Freelancers starts work
- Release escrow payments only after reviewing and approving deliverables
- Maintain transparency to avoid disputes

### 5. Keep Track of Project Timeline

- Update milestones if scope changes
- Adjust deadlines for longer or more complex deliverables
- Use the platform tools to monitor overall project progress

## Tips for Success

- Break larger projects into smaller, manageable milestones
- Maintain open and clear communication with Freelancers
- Document changes and approvals to ensure accountability
