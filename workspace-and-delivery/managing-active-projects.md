# Managing Your Active Projects on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

## What you'll achieve

- Stay organized and track your project progress efficiently
- Ensure timely delivery and maintain a strong reputation with businesses

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Freelancers account
- At least one active project or milestone
- Milestones and deliverables defined for each project

## Steps to Manage Your Active Projects

### 1. Monitor Project Milestones

Keep track of all milestones for your projects. Ensure you:

- Know the deliverable and deadline for each milestone
- Update progress regularly on the platform
- Communicate early if delays are expected

### 2. Submit Deliverables on Time

- Upload files or completed work through TechSynergy's secure system
- Confirm submissions match agreed specifications
- Use milestone checklists to ensure nothing is missed

### 3. Communicate Transparently

- Use TechSynergy's messaging tools to ask questions or clarify requirements
- Provide regular updates to the business
- Avoid off-platform communication until the project is completed

### 4. Track Payments

- Verify that milestones are funded before starting work
- Check escrow status for each milestone
- Notify the business when deliverables are submitted for approval

### 5. Handle Feedback Professionally

- Respond promptly to review comments
- Make requested revisions if needed
- Maintain professionalism to build long-term trust

## Tips for Success

- Prioritize projects by deadline and complexity
- Keep all deliverables organized in your portfolio for future use
- Treat each milestone as a mini-deadline to ensure smooth completion
