# Get Paid from Escrow After Work Approval

**Who this is for:** Freelancers / Freelancer

## What you'll achieve

- Understand how escrow guarantees your payment
- Get paid immediately after work approval with no delays

## Prerequisites

- Active Freelancers account
- Active project with a milestone funded into escrow before the project starts
- Work submitted and marked ready for review
- Wallet set up and active

## Steps

- **Confirm the milestone is funded before starting work** The business pays the milestone amount into escrow before the project begins. **Expected result:** Your payment is secured in advance.
- **Complete and submit your work** Upload deliverables and mark the milestone as ready for review. **Expected result:** The business is notified to review your work.
- **Milestone approved by the business** The business marks the milestone as **Completed**. **Expected result:** Escrow funds are released automatically.
- **Instant payment to your wallet** Because the money is already in escrow, payment cannot be delayed. **Expected result:** Funds appear immediately in your wallet.
- **Withdraw your earnings** Transfer funds from your wallet to your bank or payout method. **Expected result:** Earnings are withdrawn successfully.

## FAQ

- **Can the client delay my payment after approval?** No. Since the money is already in escrow, payment is released instantly after approval.
- **Is my payment secured before I start working?** Yes. You should only start work once the milestone is funded into escrow.
- **What if the client hasn't funded the milestone yet?** Do not start work until funding is complete.
- **When will I see the money in my wallet?** Immediately after the milestone is marked as completed.
- **Can the client take back the money after approval?** No. Once released from escrow, payment is final.

## Troubleshooting

- **Milestone shows "Not funded"**
  *Cause:* Client has not paid into escrow.
  *Fix:* Wait until the milestone is funded before starting work.
- **Wallet balance not updated**
  *Cause:* Milestone not yet approved.
  *Fix:* Check milestone status or contact support.
- **Cannot withdraw funds**
  *Cause:* Payout method not verified.
  *Fix:* Complete verification in wallet settings.
