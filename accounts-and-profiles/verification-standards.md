# Verification for Freelancers on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Understand TechSynergy's KYC (Know Your Customer) verification process
- Learn how to earn a skills verification badge to increase visibility to businesses

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Freelancers account
- Government-issued ID for KYC verification
- Skills added to your profile

## KYC Verification: Proving Your Identity

TechSynergy requires all Freelancers to complete KYC verification to ensure a safe and trustworthy platform for both Freelancer and businesses.

### How KYC Verification Works

1. **Submit Identification**
   Upload a valid government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID). Ensure the document is clear, current, and matches your account information.

2. **Verification Review**
   TechSynergy reviews your submitted ID to confirm your identity. This process protects against fraud and builds trust with businesses.

3. **Completion**
   Once approved, your account is marked as KYC Verified, signaling to businesses that you are a trusted professional.

### Benefits for Freelancer

- Builds credibility and trust with businesses
- Allows you to participate in higher-value projects
- Increases your chances of being shortlisted for opportunities

## Skills Verification Badge: Showcasing Your Expertise

TechSynergy allows Freelancer to verify their skills through a structured assessment process. Verified skills earn a Skills Verified Badge on your profile, giving businesses confidence in your abilities.

### How Skills Verification Works

1. **Request a Skills Assessment**
   Submit your CV and select the skills you want to verify.

2. **Expert Evaluation**
   A field expert reviews your CV and conducts a single skill assessment based on all the skills you present. The expert will ask questions, test practical knowledge, and provide recommendations for improvement.

3. **Earn Your Badge**
   Upon successful verification, a Skills Verified Badge is displayed on your profile. This badge highlights your expertise and increases visibility to businesses searching for verified Freelancers.

### Benefits for Freelancer

- Demonstrates verified expertise to potential clients
- Increases the likelihood of being hired for high-profile projects
- Encourages skill improvement through expert feedback

## FAQ

**What documents are accepted for KYC?**
Passport, driver's license, or national ID. Ensure they are valid and clearly visible.

**How long does verification take?**
Typically 1-3 business days, depending on document clarity and completeness.

**Can I verify all my skills at once?**
Yes. The skills assessment evaluates all the skills you present on your CV in a single review.

**Do verification badges expire?**
Skills may be reassessed if major platform updates or skill standards change. KYC verification does not expire but may require revalidation in rare cases.

**Does verification guarantee I will be hired?**
No, but verified Freelancer are more visible and trusted by businesses, increasing your chances of selection.
