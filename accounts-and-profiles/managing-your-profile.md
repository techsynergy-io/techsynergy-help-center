# Managing Your Profile on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

## What you'll achieve

- Present your skills and experience effectively to attract the right projects
- Increase your credibility and visibility to businesses

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Freelancers account
- Skills and experience added to your profile

## 1. Keep Your Skills Up-to-Date

Businesses search for Freelancer based on skills and expertise. Ensure your profile lists:

- Current skills and tools
- Relevant experience and projects
- Certifications or verified skills, if available

> **Tip:** Accurate and detailed skills help businesses quickly assess whether you're a good fit for their projects.

## 2. Showcase Your Work

Highlight your achievements, projects, and portfolio items. Include:

- Portfolio pieces or project examples
- Measurable results or accomplishments
- Testimonials or recommendations from past clients

> **Tip:** Showcasing your work builds trust and credibility before businesses even contact you.

## 3. Write a Strong Bio

Your bio is the first impression businesses get. Make it:

- Clear and concise
- Client-focused, explaining how you solve problems
- Highlighting your expertise and experience

**Example:** "Mobile developer with 3 years of experience in React Native apps for fintech startups. I help businesses build scalable apps that enhance user engagement and drive growth."

## 4. Respond Promptly to Opportunities

Even though profiles are public, your activity matters. Make sure to:

- Apply to projects that match your skills
- Respond promptly to messages from businesses
- Keep your availability status updated

> **Tip:** Being responsive improves your reputation and increases your chances of getting hired.

## 5. Build Credibility with Verifications

Optional verifications like Skills Verification and KYC (identity verification) enhance trust. Verified Freelancer often:

- Stand out in searches
- Receive more project invitations
- Increase their chances of being hired

## 6. Keep Your Profile Active

Regularly update your profile by:

- Adding new skills and experience
- Highlighting recent project outcomes or achievements
- Refreshing your bio and portfolio

> **Tip:** An active, well-maintained profile signals professionalism and reliability to businesses.
