# Verify Your Identity (KYC)

**Who this is for:** Freelancers seeking to increase profile trust

## What you'll achieve

- Confirm your identity by uploading a valid government-issued ID
- Obtain the ID Verification badge to build trust with businesses
- Enhance your profile credibility on TechSynergy

## What you need

- A scanned copy or photo of a valid government ID (passport, driver's license, national ID card)
- A small token fee payment for the verification process

## How it works

1. Upload your government-issued ID through the verification portal.
2. Pay the required token fee.
3. Your ID will be reviewed and verified by TechSynergy.
4. Once approved, the ID Verification badge will appear on your profile.

## Expected result

- Your identity is verified securely.
- You earn the ID Verification badge visible to businesses.
- Your chances of being trusted and hired increase.

## Important notes

- KYC verification is optional and not required to access your Freelancers dashboard or apply for jobs.
- You can complete this verification at any time after onboarding.

## Troubleshooting

**What if my ID is rejected?**

- Check that your ID is valid, clear, and not expired.
- Re-upload a better-quality image if needed.
- Contact support if issues persist.

**Can I request a refund for the token fee?**
Token fees are generally non-refundable. Refer to the refund policy for details.
