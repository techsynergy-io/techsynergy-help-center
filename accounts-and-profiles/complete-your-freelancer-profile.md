# Complete Your Professional Freelancer Profile

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

## What you'll achieve

- Build a high-visibility profile that attracts top-tier business clients.
- Reach "Profile Complete" status to unlock the project marketplace.

## Prerequisites

- A verified TechSynergy account.
- Digital copies of your portfolio or links to live projects (GitHub, Behance, etc.).

## Steps

1. **Add a Professional Bio:** Write a concise summary of your technical expertise.
2. **Define Your Skills:** Select at least 5 core competencies (e.g., Python, AWS, React).
3. **Link Your Portfolio:** Add URLs to your external work.
4. **Set Your Availability:** Indicate if you're available or busy.

## FAQ

**What counts as a "valid" portfolio link?**
GitHub repos, live web apps, or PDF case studies.

**Is a profile picture mandatory?**
No, although a clear professional headshot would be great for trust.

**How often should I update my profile?**
We recommend every 3 months or after a major project.

## Troubleshooting

**Profile stuck at 90% completion:**
Ensure you have added at least one "Work Experience" entry.
