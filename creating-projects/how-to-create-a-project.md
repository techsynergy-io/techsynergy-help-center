# How to create a project (step-by-step)

**Who this is for:** Client

### What you'll achieve:

- Successfully **post a project** to attract high-quality applicants.
- Define your project scope and structural requirements to find the perfect **Freelancers**.

### Prerequisites:

- A registered Business profile.
- A clear understanding of the project's technical goals.

### Steps:

- **Initiate Posting:** Click the "Post a Project" button on your Business Dashboard.
- **Define the Scope:** Select the project size (Small/Quick, Medium/Standard, or Large/Complex).
- **Select Industry & Category:** Choose the primary industry (e.g., Fintech, Healthcare) and the technical domain (e.g., AI/ML, Frontend).
- **Set Project Type:** Choose between **Fixed-Price (Milestone)** or **Hourly**.
- **Assign Roles/Seats:** Define how many experts you need to **hire developers** for (e.g., 2 Backend Devs, 1 QA Engineer).
- **Review and Launch:** Preview the listing and click "Publish." **Expected Result:** Your project becomes visible to matched Freelancers in the marketplace.

### FAQ:

- **How much does it cost to post?** Posting is free; you only pay when milestones are funded or hours are logged.
- **Can I change the scope later?** You can edit the description before publsishing, but fundamental changes to the payment type may require a new post.
- **What is "Difficulty Level"?** A rating (Entry, Intermediate, Expert) that helps filter the Freelancer pool for your specific budget and quality needs.
- **How long does a project stay active?** Projects stay open for 30 days unless filled or manually closed earlier.
- **Can I invite specific Freelancers?** Yes, you can browse profiles and click "Invite to Project" once your post is live.

### Troubleshooting:

- **Project rejected during review:** Ensure your description doesn't contain prohibited content or off-platform payment requests.
- **No applicants after 24 hours:** Try broadening your "Nice-to-have" skills or adjusting your budget to market rates.
- **"Role not found":** If a specific niche role isn't listed, select the closest category and specify details in the brief.
