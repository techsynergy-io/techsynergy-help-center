# Writing Effective Project Information

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Provide clear project information that attracts the right Freelancers
- Ensure professionals understand your goals, deliverables, and expectations

## Prerequisites

- An active Business account on TechSynergy
- A clear understanding of your project goals, scope, and timeline

## Why Clear Project Information Matters

The **Project Information** section bridges your business objectives and the professional who will execute your project. Well-defined details reduce misunderstandings, attract qualified Freelancers, and speed up project delivery.

## Steps to Filling Out Project Information

### 1. Project Title: Be Specific and Action-Oriented

*Expected result:* Freelancers instantly understands the project focus.

- Avoid vague titles like "Marketing Help" or "Website Task."
- Include technology, platform, or primary goal in the title.

**Good Examples:**

- "Email Marketing Campaign for Q1 Product Launch"
- "Python Backend Optimization for High-Traffic E-commerce Site"

**Tip:** Focus on the outcome or technology that defines the project.

### 2. Project Roles & Category

*Expected result:* Your project reaches the right Freelancers pool.

- **Project Roles:** Select the expertise you need (e.g., UI Designer, Data Analyst, Lead Copywriter).
- **Project Category:** Pick the most relevant industry or service category so Freelancers can filter for your project.

### 3. Writing the Project Description

*Expected result:* Freelancers understands the scope, expectations, and required skills. Include these three key elements:

- **Context:** Briefly explain your business and why this project matters.
- **Deliverables:** Clearly define what you expect to receive (e.g., "A 5-page responsive website," "A 2,000-word whitepaper," "A fully audited codebase").
- **Required Skills:** List must-have tools, platforms, or languages (e.g., "Experience with React," "Familiarity with SEO best practices").

### 4. Pro-Tips for Strong Project Information

*Expected result:* Your project details are clear, actionable, and appealing to Freelancers.

- **Keep it concise but comprehensive:** Use bullet points for easy scanning.
- **Define success:** Include measurable outcomes if possible (e.g., "We aim to reduce page load speed by 20%").
- **Be clear about expectations:** Mention timelines, milestones, or deliverable formats.
