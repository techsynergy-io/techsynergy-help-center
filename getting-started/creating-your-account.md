# Creating Your TechSynergy Account

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Create a Freelancers account on TechSynergy
- Choose a preferred sign-in method
- Verify your email address
- Proceed to onboarding to complete your profile

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- A Google account (if choosing Google authentication)

## How Freelancers account creation works

Freelancers can create a TechSynergy account using either Google authentication or an email address and password. Regardless of the method chosen, email verification is required before full access is granted.

## Steps

### 1. Start account creation

- Go to the TechSynergy sign-up page.
- Choose one of the following options:
  - Sign up using Google authentication
  - Sign up using your email address and a password

### 2. Verify your email

- A verification email will be sent to your registered email address.
- Open the email and follow the instructions to verify your account.

### 3. Access your account

- Once verified, you can sign in to your Freelancers account using your chosen method.

### 4. Complete onboarding

- After sign-up, you will be guided to the onboarding stage.
- You'll be required to provide additional personal, professional, and profile information to complete your setup.

## Expected result

- Your Freelancers account is created and verified.
- You are ready to complete onboarding and start using TechSynergy.

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the verification email

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Make sure you used the correct email address.
- Request a new verification email if needed.

### I chose the wrong sign-in method

- You can continue using the method selected during sign-up or contact support if changes are required.
