# Logging In and Account Access

**Who this is for:** Freelancers and Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Access your TechSynergy account securely
- Keep your profile, messages, and data protected

## Prerequisites

- An active TechSynergy account (Freelancers or Business)
- Access to the email address registered to your account

## How it works

All TechSynergy accounts use email-based verification for secure sign-in. Each time you log in, a one-time password (OTP) is sent to your registered email address. You must enter the OTP before access is granted.

This applies to both Freelancers and Business accounts.

## Steps

### 1. Begin sign-in

- Go to the TechSynergy sign-in page.
- Enter the email address associated with your account.

### 2. Verify your email

- Check your registered email for the OTP.
- The OTP is valid for a limited time and can only be used once.

### 3. Complete sign-in

- Enter the OTP on the sign-in screen to access your account.

## Expected result

- You successfully sign in to your TechSynergy account.
- Your account remains secure and protected against unauthorized access.

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the OTP email

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Ensure you entered the correct registered email address.
- Request a new OTP if necessary.

### The OTP expired

- Request a new OTP and enter it promptly.

### I no longer have access to my registered email

- Contact TechSynergy support for account recovery assistance.
