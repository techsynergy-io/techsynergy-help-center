# Welcome to TechSynergy: Platform Overview

**Who this is for:** Clients and Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Understand how TechSynergy connects Freelancers with high-impact projects.
- Learn the core principles of our milestone-based workflow and high-trust ecosystem.

## Prerequisites

- A desire to either build products or provide technical expertise in roles like Developer, Designer, or Data Analyst.

## Steps

1. **Identify Your Role:** Choose between being a Freelancer (showcasing skills) or a Business (hiring for a project).
2. **Explore Popular Categories:** Review the platform's core domains, including Mobile Dev, Product Manager, and UI/UX Design.
3. **Understand Project Delivery:** Projects are completed by Freelancers with built-in management tools to track progress.
4. **Adopt Trust & Safety Principles:** All users agree to a framework of verification, content moderation, and fraud prevention to ensure a safe environment.

## FAQ

### Who is TechSynergy for?

It is for individual Freelancers and businesses ranging from individual project owners, startups to large enterprises.

### How do payments work?

We use a Quality Assurance system to ensure funds are only released for verified, high-quality work.

### Is my data safe?

Yes, we prioritize Data Security through encryption and strict access controls.

### What is the platform's reputation?

We maintain a 4.9/5 rating based on thousands of professional interactions.

### What if a project goes wrong?

Our Trust and Safety team provides dispute resolution and accountability for all parties.

## Troubleshooting

### Role confusion

If you aren't sure which role to pick, remember that "Freelancer" is for workers and "I have a project" is for hirers.

### Safety concerns

Report any suspicious behavior immediately to our moderation team via the support portal.
