# Completing Onboarding on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Businesses and individuals posting projects

## What you'll achieve

- Complete required onboarding after account creation
- Set up your profile correctly based on how you plan to post projects
- Gain access to your Business dashboard

## When this happens

Onboarding begins immediately after you create and verify your account. This stage is mandatory and must be completed before you can access your dashboard.

## How Business onboarding works

During onboarding, you'll first choose how you want to use TechSynergy:

- As an **Individual** posting projects
- As a **Business** posting projects

Based on your selection, you'll be guided through short forms to collect the right details.

## Steps

### 1. Choose your account type

Select one of the following options:

- **Individual** -- for solo professionals posting personal projects
- **Business** -- for companies or teams posting projects

### 2. Fill in basic details

- Complete a short form with required information.
- This includes contact details and location information.

### 3. Complete profile information

**If you selected Business:**

- Provide your company details, such as company name, email, address, and industry.
- Add a brief description of what your company does.

**If you selected Individual:**

- Provide personal details about yourself.
- This information helps set up your individual project-posting profile.

### 4. Finish onboarding

- Submit the required information to complete onboarding.

## Expected result

- Your onboarding is completed successfully.
- You gain full access to your Business dashboard.
- You're ready to post projects and use TechSynergy.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't skip onboarding

- Onboarding is required and must be completed before dashboard access is granted.

### I selected the wrong account type

- You may be able to update this later in your account settings or contact support for help.
