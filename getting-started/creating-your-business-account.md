# Creating Your TechSynergy Account

**Who this is for:** Businesses and individuals posting projects

## What you'll achieve

- Create a TechSynergy account to post projects
- Verify your email address
- Complete onboarding to choose how you want to post projects

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- Ability to create and manage a secure password

## How Business account creation works

Business accounts are created using an email address and password. After signing up, you must verify your email before continuing. Once verified, you'll move to onboarding, where additional details are collected.

## Steps

### 1. Start account creation

- Go to the TechSynergy sign-up page.
- Enter your email address.
- Create a secure password.

### 2. Verify your email

- A verification email will be sent to your registered email address.
- Open the email and follow the instructions to verify your account.

### 3. Access your account

- Once verified, you can sign in to your account.

### 4. Complete onboarding

- After sign-up, you will be guided to the onboarding stage.
- During onboarding, you'll choose how you want to post projects:
  - **Individual** -- for solo professionals or personal project posting
  - **Business** -- for companies or teams posting projects
- You'll also be asked to provide additional details based on your selection.

## Expected result

- Your account is created and verified.
- Your onboarding is completed with the correct posting setup.
- You're ready to post projects on TechSynergy.

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the verification email

- Check your spam or junk folder.
- Confirm you entered the correct email address.
- Request a new verification email if available.

### I chose the wrong option during onboarding

- Update your selection in your account settings or contact support for help.
