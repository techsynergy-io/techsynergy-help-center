# Completing Onboarding on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Complete your Freelancers profile setup
- Provide basic information to create your account
- Complete skill assessments
- Optional: portfolio uploads and verifications
- Gain access to your Freelancers dashboard

## When this happens

Onboarding starts right after you create and verify your Freelancers account. Basic information must be completed before accessing your dashboard. Skill assessment, portfolio upload, and verification are optional steps you can complete later.

## How Freelancers onboarding works

Your onboarding journey includes four main stages:

### 1. Basic Information (Compulsory)

- Provide essential personal details such as name, email, phone number, and location.
- This forms the foundation of your Freelancers profile.

### 2. Skill Assessment (Optional)

- Complete tests to demonstrate your skills.
- Helps showcase your expertise to potential clients.

### 3. Portfolio Upload (Optional)

- Add examples of your work or projects to strengthen your profile.

### 4. Verification (Optional)

- **Skill Verification:** Request an expert interview to validate your skills.
- **KYC Verification:** Upload a valid ID card for identity confirmation.
- These steps increase your profile credibility but are not required to start using the platform.

## Steps to complete onboarding

1. Fill in your basic information.
2. Complete skill assessments.
3. (Optional) Upload your portfolio.
4. (Optional) Submit verification requests.
5. Submit your basic info to unlock your dashboard.

## Expected result

- Your Freelancers profile is created with your basic details.
- You can immediately access your Freelancers dashboard and start applying for jobs.
- Completing optional steps later can improve your visibility and trust on the platform.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't access my dashboard

- Ensure your basic information form is fully completed and submitted.

### Can I skip skill and KYC verification?

- Yes, these are optional and can be done anytime later.

### I need help with portfolio uploads or verification

- Visit our dedicated help articles or contact support for guidance.
