# Report Unauthorized Account Activity

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Report suspicious or unauthorized activity on your Business account
- Help secure your company profile, communications, and payments from further misuse

## Prerequisites

- An active Business account
- Access to the email address linked to your account
- Details of the suspicious activity (dates, actions, screenshots if available)

## Steps

### 1. Secure your account

**Expected result:** Reduced risk of further unauthorized access.

- Change your account password immediately.
- Ensure only authorized team members have access to your account credentials.

### 2. Review recent account activity

**Expected result:** You identify what may be unauthorized.

- Review your business profile details, messages, job postings, contracts, and payment activity.
- Take note of anything you don't recognize or didn't approve.

### 3. Gather relevant details

**Expected result:** Your report is clear and actionable.

Include:

- Your business name and account email
- A description of what happened and when it happened
- Screenshots or supporting evidence, if available

### 4. Send an email report

**Expected result:** Your case is received for review.

- Email **jemmima@techsynergy.io**
- Use the subject line: **"Unauthorized Account Activity -- Business"**

### 5. Monitor your email

**Expected result:** You receive next steps or follow-up questions.

- Respond promptly to help resolve the issue faster.

## FAQ

**What counts as unauthorized activity?**
Any action you didn't approve, such as messages sent from your account, changes to your business profile, unauthorized job postings, or suspicious payment activity.

**What if I'm not sure the activity was unauthorized?**
Report it anyway. Early investigation helps prevent further risk.

**Can my account be temporarily restricted?**
Yes. Temporary restrictions may be applied to protect your account during the investigation.

**How long does it take to get a response?**
Response times vary, but reports are reviewed as quickly as possible.

## Troubleshooting

- **I secured my account but activity continues**
  Fix: Email the report immediately with updated details.
- **I don't remember exact dates or actions**
  Fix: Provide approximate times and descriptions.
- **I didn't receive a response**
  Fix: Allow some time, then send a polite follow-up email.
