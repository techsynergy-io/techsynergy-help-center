# Safety and Fraud Prevention Tips

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Learn how to safely engage and hire Freelancer
- Protect your projects, payments, and account

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Business account

### Keep All Communication on the Platform

Always communicate with Freelancer through TechSynergy messaging tools. This ensures a record of conversations, helps resolve disputes, and prevents off-platform scams. Avoid email or personal messaging until a project is formally agreed.

### Fund Escrow Before Awarding Projects

Ensure the project is funded through TechSynergy's escrow system before awarding work. Use milestones to manage payments securely. This protects both parties and ensures fair transactions.

### Verify Freelancers Profiles

Check verification badges (KYC and skills), review ratings and past projects, and ensure the Freelancers's experience matches your project requirements. Ask questions if something is unclear.

### Protect Sensitive Information

Do not request passwords, personal account details, or off-platform payments. TechSynergy will never require you to transfer money directly to individuals outside the platform.

### Define Clear Scope and Milestones

Confirm deliverables, set realistic timelines, and agree on payment milestones before work begins. Clear agreements reduce misunderstandings and disputes.

### Watch for Red Flags

Be alert to urgent demands, unclear or changing project requirements, requests to bypass platform processes, or offers that seem too good to be true. Pause if something feels suspicious.

### Report Suspicious Activity

Report suspected fraud, fake profiles, harassment, or misleading listings immediately using TechSynergy's reporting tools. Early reporting helps protect the entire community.

### Protect Your Account

Use a strong, unique password, enable available security features, and never share login details. Account security is a shared responsibility.

**Creating a Secure Marketplace**
By following these guidelines, businesses can safely hire and collaborate while contributing to a trusted and transparent environment on TechSynergy.
