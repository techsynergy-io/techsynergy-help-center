# Report Unauthorized Account Activity

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Report suspicious or unauthorized activity on your Freelancers account
- Help secure your profile, messages, and earnings from further misuse

## Prerequisites

- An active Freelancers account
- Access to the email address linked to your account
- Details of the suspicious activity (dates, actions, screenshots if available)

## Steps

- **Secure your account**
  *Expected result:* Reduced risk of further unauthorized access.
  - If you use **Google sign-in**, secure your Google account by changing your Google password and reviewing recent sign-ins.
  - If you use email and password, change your password immediately.
- **Review recent account activity**
  *Expected result:* You identify what may be unauthorized.
  - Check your profile details, messages, job applications, and earnings activity.
  - Note anything you don't recognize.
- **Gather relevant details**
  *Expected result:* Your report is clear and actionable.
  - Your full name and account email
  - What happened and when it happened
  - Screenshots or evidence, if available
- **Send an email report**
  *Expected result:* Your case is received for review.
  - Email **jemmima@techsynergy.io**
  - Use the subject line: **"Unauthorized Account Activity -- Freelancers"**
- **Monitor your email**
  *Expected result:* You receive next steps or follow-up questions.
  - Respond promptly to help resolve the issue faster.

## FAQ

**What counts as unauthorized activity?**
Anything you didn't approve, such as messages sent from your account, profile changes, or suspicious earnings activity.

**I use Google sign-in. Do I still need to report issues here?**
Yes. Secure your Google account first, then report the issue to TechSynergy.

**What if I'm not sure the activity was unauthorized?**
Report it anyway. It's better to investigate early.

**Can my account be temporarily restricted?**
Yes, to protect your account during the investigation.

**How long does it take to get a response?**
Response times vary, but reports are reviewed as quickly as possible.

## Troubleshooting

- **I secured my Google account but activity continues**
  Fix: Email the report immediately with updated details.
- **I don't remember exact dates or actions**
  Fix: Provide approximate times and descriptions.
- **I didn't receive a response**
  Fix: Allow some time, then send a polite follow-up email.
