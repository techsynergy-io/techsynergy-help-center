# Safety and Fraud Prevention Tips

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

## What you'll achieve

- Understand how to stay safe while working on TechSynergy
- Learn best practices to avoid scams and protect your account

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Freelancers account
- Completed KYC verification recommended

### Keep All Communication on the Platform

Always use TechSynergy's messaging tools to communicate with businesses. This creates a record of conversations, helps resolve disputes, and reduces the risk of off-platform scams. Avoid moving discussions to email, messaging apps, or phone calls before a project is formally agreed.

### Use Escrow Before Starting Work

Never begin work unless the project has been properly funded through TechSynergy's escrow system. Confirm escrow is funded before starting and do not accept promises of "later payment."

### Verify Business Profiles

Review business profiles carefully: check KYC and other verification badges, read ratings and past reviews, and ensure the project scope matches their description. Ask questions if anything seems unclear.

### Protect Personal Information

TechSynergy will never ask you to share passwords or sensitive account details, or to accept off-platform payments. Treat any request for this information as suspicious.

### Agree on Clear Scope and Milestones

Before starting work, confirm deliverables, set realistic timelines, and agree on milestones for payment release. Clear agreements reduce misunderstandings and disputes.

### Watch for Red Flags

Be cautious of urgent requests, offers that seem too good to be true, changing project requirements, or requests to bypass platform processes. Pause and verify if something feels off.

### Report Suspicious Activity

Immediately report suspected fraud, harassment, fake profiles, or misleading listings using TechSynergy's reporting tools. Early reporting helps protect the community.

### Protect Your Account

Use a strong, unique password, enable additional security features when available, and never share login details. Account security is a shared responsibility.

**Building a Safer Community Together**
Following these tips helps you work confidently while contributing to a safer TechSynergy environment for all Freelancer and businesses.
