# How to Improve Your Skill Score

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Increase your overall skill score
- Improve how your abilities are represented on the spider graph

## Prerequisites

- An active Freelancers profile
- At least one skill added to your profile
- Ability to edit and update your skills

## How Skill Scores Work

Your skill score is driven by **skill points**. Each point you add increases the strength of that skill and pushes it farther out on the spider graph. Higher points also move a skill into higher proficiency levels, improving your overall score and visibility.

## Steps to Improve Your Score

1. **Add all relevant skills**
   Include both core and supporting skills. Don't limit yourself to just one area if you have real experience in others.
   *Expected result:* Your profile reflects your full capability set.

2. **Increase points in your strongest skills**
   Focus on skills you actively use or have strong experience in. Higher points push skills toward Advanced or Expert levels.
   *Expected result:* Clear expertise appears on the spider graph.

3. **Balance related skills**
   Closely related skills should not be drastically uneven. Balanced growth improves the overall shape of the spider graph.
   *Expected result:* A more complete and credible skill profile.

4. **Remove or lower outdated skills**
   Reducing unused skills prevents score dilution. Accuracy is more important than quantity.
   *Expected result:* Your score reflects current abilities.

5. **Update skills regularly**
   Revisit your skills as you complete projects or learn new tools. Increment points when your proficiency genuinely improves.
   *Expected result:* Your score grows with your experience.

6. **Aim for higher proficiency levels**
   Moving skills from Beginner to Intermediate or Advanced has a visible impact. Expert-level skills significantly boost your profile strength.
   *Expected result:* Stronger profile signals to businesses.

## FAQ

**Does adding more skills automatically increase my score?**
Not always. Adding skills helps, but increasing points in relevant skills has a bigger impact.

**Is it better to specialize or be well-rounded?**
It depends on your goals. Specialists stand out for niche roles, while well-rounded profiles suit general roles.

**Can I lose points or score?**
Yes. Lowering or removing skill points will reduce your score accordingly.

**How often should I update my skills?**
Whenever you gain meaningful experience or complete relevant work.

**Do businesses see my exact points?**
They see your proficiency levels and the spider graph, not raw point values.

## Troubleshooting

- **My score isn't improving**
  Fix: Increase points in existing skills rather than only adding new ones.

- **My spider graph looks uneven**
  Fix: Strengthen weak but related skills or focus intentionally on specialization.

- **I added skills but see little change**
  Fix: Ensure skills have enough points to move into higher proficiency levels.
