# Taking Your First Skill Assessment

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Get your skills reviewed and verified by an industry expert
- Earn a verified skill badge that increases visibility to businesses

## Prerequisites

- An active Freelancers account
- A completed profile with skills added
- An up-to-date CV or resume uploaded or ready to share

## What a Skill Assessment Is

A skill assessment is a structured evaluation requested by a Freelancer to validate their listed skills. Once requested, an **expert in the relevant field** is scheduled to conduct a one-on-one interview.

During the session, your CV is reviewed and your skills are tested through discussion, practical questions, or task-based evaluation. The expert also provides **personalized recommendations** for improving your skills.

At the end of a successful assessment, you receive a **Verified Skill badge**, showing that your skills have been reviewed by an expert. This badge increases your **credibility and visibility to businesses** looking for trusted Freelancers.

## Steps

1. **Request a skill assessment**
   Select the skill or skill category you want assessed from your profile.
   *Expected result:* Your request is submitted for review.

2. **Get matched with an expert**
   An expert in the field is assigned. You'll be notified of the interview date and time.
   *Expected result:* An assessment interview is scheduled.

3. **Prepare for the assessment**
   Review your CV and past projects. Be prepared to explain how you've used your listed skills.
   *Expected result:* You're ready to demonstrate your experience.

4. **Attend the assessment interview**
   The expert reviews your CV. Your skills are tested through questions, scenarios, or live exercises.
   *Expected result:* Your skills are evaluated and validated.

5. **Receive feedback and improvement recommendations**
   The expert explains your current proficiency level. You receive actionable recommendations to improve specific skills.
   *Expected result:* Clear guidance on strengths and growth areas.

6. **Earn your verified skill badge**
   Verified skills are marked with a badge on your profile. Businesses can easily identify you as expert-reviewed Freelancers.
   *Expected result:* Your profile gains verified status and higher visibility.

## FAQ

**Who conducts the skill assessment?**
An experienced professional with expertise in the relevant field.

**Is this a pass-or-fail assessment?**
No. The goal is skill validation and improvement, not elimination.

**Where does the verified badge appear?**
The badge appears on your skill list and profile, visible to businesses.

**Does the badge affect how businesses find me?**
Yes. Verified skills improve trust and increase your visibility in searches.

**How long does the assessment take?**
Typically between 30 and 60 minutes.

**Can I retake or update an assessment later?**
Yes. You can request reassessment as your skills improve.

## Troubleshooting

- **My assessment hasn't been scheduled yet**
  Fix: Allow time for expert matching. You'll be notified once scheduled.

- **I missed my interview**
  Fix: Request a reschedule as soon as possible.

- **I didn't receive my badge**
  Fix: Badges are applied after expert review. Contact support if delayed.

- **I'm unsure how to apply the feedback**
  Fix: Use the recommendations as a learning roadmap and update skills as you grow.
