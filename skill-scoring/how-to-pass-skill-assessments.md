# How to Pass Verification Skill Assessments on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Showcase your expertise and earn verified skills recognition
- Increase credibility with businesses and improve hiring prospects

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Freelancers account
- Skills added to your profile
- Updated CV and portfolio

## Step 1: Request a Skill Assessment

Request a skill assessment through your TechSynergy profile to have your skills formally verified. Focus on the skills you are confident in, as this increases your chances of success.

## Step 2: Prepare Your CV and Portfolio

Before the assessment, ensure your CV and portfolio are up-to-date and organized. Include:

- Relevant projects and achievements
- Technologies or tools you are proficient in
- Certifications, training, or courses completed

**Tip:** Highlight examples where your skills made a measurable impact. Businesses value results and practical experience.

## Step 3: Schedule the Expert Interview

After your request is accepted, a field expert will schedule an interview to evaluate your skills. The interview typically covers:

- Your professional experience and background
- Specific technical or domain skills
- Your approach to projects and problem-solving

**Tip:** Treat this like a professional job interview. Be ready to clearly explain your work and thought process.

## Step 4: Demonstrate Your Skills

During the interview:

- Be honest and clear about your abilities
- Explain your workflow and methods
- Share examples from past projects to illustrate practical experience
- Ask clarifying questions if a scenario isn't clear

The goal is to show that your skills match your profile and meet professional standards.

## Step 5: Follow Up After the Assessment

After the interview, the expert may provide feedback or request additional information. Respond promptly and professionally.

**Tip:** Use feedback to improve your skills and profile, even if you don't pass on the first attempt.

## Step 6: Showcase Your Verified Skills

Once your skills are verified:

- A **Skills Verified badge** appears on your profile
- Businesses can see your abilities are confirmed by an expert
- Your credibility and chances of getting hired increase significantly
