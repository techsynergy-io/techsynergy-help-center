# Funding Your First Milestone on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Learn how to fund milestones securely and build trust with Freelancer
- Ensure smooth project delivery with clear payment structures

## Prerequisites

- Active TechSynergy Business account
- Defined project with agreed milestones
- Project budget allocated for escrow funding

## What is a Milestone?

A milestone is a specific, agreed-upon part of your project with a clear deliverable. Milestones help:

- Break larger projects into manageable steps
- Track progress and measure success
- Tie payments directly to completed work

## Why Funding Your First Milestone Matters

Funding your first milestone through TechSynergy's escrow system ensures:

- Freelancer know payment is secured before starting work
- Deliverables are linked to secure payment releases
- Projects stay on schedule and minimize disputes

## Steps to Fund Your First Milestone

### 1. Define the Milestone

Clearly describe the deliverable, expected outcomes, deadlines, and success criteria.

### 2. Set the Amount

Decide the portion of the total project fee that corresponds to this milestone.

### 3. Fund the Milestone via Escrow

Deposit the milestone amount into TechSynergy's escrow account. Funds are held securely and released only when the milestone is successfully completed.

### 4. Communicate with the Freelancers

Confirm the milestone scope, deadlines, and expectations. Transparent communication builds trust and reduces misunderstandings.

### 5. Release Payment Upon Completion

Once the Freelancers delivers the milestone and you're satisfied with the work, release the payment through TechSynergy.

## Best Practices for Milestone Funding

- Start with smaller initial milestones for new Freelancer to reduce risk
- Clearly outline expectations, deliverables, and success criteria
- Maintain open communication throughout the process
- Document any changes to scope or deadlines within the platform

## Benefits of Using Milestone Funding

- **Security:** Funds are safely held in escrow
- **Accountability:** Freelancer are motivated to complete tasks on time
- **Trust:** Builds confidence between businesses and Freelancer
- **Transparency:** Payments are linked to project progress
