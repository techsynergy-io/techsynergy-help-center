# Selecting Freelancer & Starting Work (Kickoff)

This guide outlines how to finalize your selection process, secure your project through initial funding, and officially signal to your Freelancer that work can begin.

**Who this is for:** Clients
**What you'll achieve:**

- Select one freelancers for your project role.
- Understand the **fund project** process and how escrow protects your investment.
- Formally initiate the work phase with a confirmed kickoff.

### Prerequisites

- An active project post with at least one applicant.
- A verified payment method on file.
- Agreement with the Freelancer on the scope of the first milestone or the hourly rate.

### Steps: Selecting Freelancer and Funding Kickoff

- **Select Your Freelancer:** Navigate to your project dashboard and click "Hire" next to the chosen applicant. If your project has multiple "seats," you can hire multiple freelancers for the same project.
- **Review the Offer:** Define the contract terms. For Milestone projects, set the amount for "Milestone 1." For Hourly, confirm the weekly limit.
- **Fund the Kickoff:** To finalize the contract, you must **fund the project**.
  - **For Milestone:** You will make an **escrow deposit** for the first milestone.
  - **For Hourly:** You will typically be charged for a deposit equivalent to the first week's limit or a platform-specific "safety" deposit.
- **Review Invoice Breakdown:** Before clicking "Pay," review the breakdown, which includes the Freelancer's fee, platform service fees, and any applicable taxes.
- **Confirm and Notify:** Once payment is processed, the system sends an automated "Start Work" notification to the Freelancer.
- **Expected Result:** The contract status moves to "Active," and the Freelancer is granted access to the project workspace.

### Understanding Kickoff Funding

When you **fund project** milestones, you are not paying the freelancer directly yet. You are placing funds into a secure **escrow deposit**.

- **What you are paying for:** You are securing the freelancer's time and demonstrating "proof of funds."
- **Escrow Protection:** The funds are held by the platform. They are only released to the Freelancer once you click "Approve" on the submitted work.
- **Start-Work Signal:** Professionals are advised **not** to begin work until they see the "Milestone Funded" status. This protects both parties.

### FAQ

- **Is the milestone deposit a setup fee?** No, the deposit is usually the payment for the first actual piece of work (e.g., "Drafting the UI Wireframe").
- **Can I get a refund on an escrow deposit?** Yes, if the work is not started or if a dispute is resolved in your favor, funds can be returned to your original payment method.
- **Does the freelancer see my credit card info?** Never. All financial transactions are handled securely by the platform.
- **Where do I track the status of my funded milestones?** Under the "Active Projectss" tab, you will see a progress bar for each funded milestone.

### Troubleshooting

- **Error: "Transaction Declined"**
  - **Fix:** Verify your daily spending limit with your bank or ensure your CVV/Expiry date is updated.
- **Freelancer says they can't start work**
  - **Fix:** Check if the milestone status is "Funded." If it says "Draft" or "Pending," the payment did not go through.
- **Hired the wrong person by mistake**
  - **Fix:** Close the contract immediately. If no work has been logged/submitted, the escrow deposit is fully refundable.
