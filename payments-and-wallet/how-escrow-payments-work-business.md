# How Escrow Payments Work on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Businesses

## What you'll achieve

- Ensure funds are secured and released based on agreed milestones
- Attract serious, committed Freelancers while reducing disputes

## Prerequisites

- Active Business account on TechSynergy
- Defined project scope, deliverables, and milestones

### 1. Fund the Escrow Account

Before posting a project, deposit the agreed project fee into TechSynergy's escrow account.

**Benefits:**

- Confirms you are financially ready to hire
- Assures Freelancers that payment is secured
- Allows you to post the project for applications

### 2. Receive Freelancers Applications

Freelancer apply knowing that:

- Payment is guaranteed through escrow
- Funds are held securely by TechSynergy
- They can focus on delivering work without payment uncertainty

### 3. Start Work With Agreed Milestones

Confirm with the Freelancers:

- Project scope
- Deliverables
- Milestones (checkpoints for progress and payment)

### 4. Review Completed Milestones

When a Freelancers submits a milestone:

- Review the deliverables
- Approve the milestone if it meets your agreed standards

### 5. Release Payment

Once a milestone is approved, TechSynergy releases the corresponding payment from escrow to the Freelancers automatically.

**Why Escrow Matters for Businesses:**

- Ensures serious, committed Freelancer apply
- Reduces disputes and misunderstandings
- Encourages structured project delivery
