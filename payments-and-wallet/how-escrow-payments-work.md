# How Escrow Payments Work on TechSynergy

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Know your payment is guaranteed before starting work
- Work confidently on projects with reduced risk

## Prerequisites

- Active Freelancers account on TechSynergy
- Project scope, deliverables, and milestones agreed with the business

### 1. Apply to Escrow-Funded Projects

When you see a project funded through escrow:

- Funds are already secured by TechSynergy
- Payment is guaranteed once milestones are approved
- You can apply confidently without worrying about non-payment

### 2. Agree on Scope and Milestones

Before starting work, confirm with the business:

- Project scope
- Deliverables
- Milestones

This ensures clear expectations and fair terms.

### 3. Complete and Submit Milestones

Finish the agreed tasks and submit each milestone for review by the business.

### 4. Receive Timely Payment

Once the business approves the milestone, TechSynergy automatically releases the corresponding payment from escrow to your account.

**Why Escrow Matters for Freelancer:**

- Eliminates fear of non-payment
- Builds trust with businesses before starting work
- Lets you focus entirely on delivering high-quality results
