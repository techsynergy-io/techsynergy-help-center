# Fund your wallet

**Who this is for:** Business
**What you'll achieve:**

- Add money to your wallet securely
- Understand payment states and invoices

**Prerequisites:**

- Business account
- At least one saved payment method

## Steps

- Go to **Billing > Wallets > Top ups**
- Select a payment method. *Supported methods depend on your region and provider (e.g., card, bank transfer).*
- Enter the amount to fund
- Confirm payment. *Funds may appear as **Pending** while processing.*
- Download receipt or invoice. *Available once the charge completes.*

## Payment states explained

- **Pending:** Payment initiated but not yet confirmed
- **Completed:** Funds added to your available balance
- **Failed:** Payment was declined or interrupted

## Troubleshooting

- **Charge failed:** Try another payment method or contact your bank.
- **Pending too long:** Bank transfers may take up to 24 hours.
