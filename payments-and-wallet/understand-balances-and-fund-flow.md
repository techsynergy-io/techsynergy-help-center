# Understand your balances and fund flow

**Who this is for:** Business
**What you'll achieve:**

- Understand how money moves from funding to payout
- Clearly distinguish between available and held funds

**Prerequisites:**

- Active business account
- At least one published project or milestone

## Steps

- Open **Wallet** from your dashboard. *You'll see your total balance and a breakdown by status.*
- Review **Available balance**. *This is money you've already added and can immediately use to fund new milestones or projects.*
- Review **Held balance (escrow)**. *These funds are locked to specific milestones and cannot be reused or withdrawn until released or refunded.*
- Open **Transaction history**. *Each transaction shows amount, status (pending, held, released), date, and related project.*
- Click into a transaction. *See when funds were funded, locked, approved, or paid out.*

## FAQ

- **What does "held" mean?** Held funds are locked in escrow for a specific milestone and cannot be moved until that milestone is resolved.
- **Can I move held funds to another project?** No. Held funds are reserved for the milestone they were funded for.
- **When do held funds unlock?** When you approve completed work, cancel the milestone, or a dispute is resolved.
- **Is escrow optional?** No. Escrow is required to protect both parties.
- **Does my wallet balance expire?** No, available funds do not expire.

## Troubleshooting

- **Balance looks incorrect:** Check for pending payments or partially funded milestones.
- **Funds stuck in escrow:** Verify milestone status or dispute state.
