# Application statuses (what each status means)

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

### What you'll achieve

- Track your progress through the hiring pipeline with confidence.
- Understand exactly where you stand with every potential client.

### Prerequisites

- At least one active Project submitted.

### Steps

- **Open "My Projects":** Navigate to the "Applications" section of your dashboard.
- **Locate the Status Label:** Each project card will display one of the following terms.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Applied | Your Project is in the client's inbox but has not been opened. |
| Shortlisted | The Business has flagged your profile for further review or an interview. |
| Accepted | You have signed the contract; work is ready to begin. |

### FAQ

- **Can I still message a Business if I am Rejected?** Yes, the chat function is enabled for all freelancers and Businesses.
- **Can I withdraw an "Applied" Project?** Yes, you can withdraw at any time to recover your application limit.
- **If I am not hired for a project I applied for, will it hurt my credibility?** No, it does not impact your Skill Matrix.
