# Platform Communication Guidelines

**Who this is for:** Freelancers

## What you'll achieve

- Communicate professionally and securely on TechSynergy
- Protect your personal information and maintain trust with clients

## Prerequisites

- An active Freelancers account
- Access to platform messaging and notifications

## Guidelines

- **Use professional and courteous language**
  *Expected result:* Clients perceive you as reliable and skilled.
  - Avoid slang, uncounted words, or offensive language.
  - Keep your messages clear and respectful at all times.

- **Keep personal contact information private**
  *Expected result:* Your privacy and safety are protected.
  - Do not share email addresses, phone numbers, or social media profiles.
  - All communication should remain on TechSynergy's messaging system.

- **Respond promptly and responsibly**
  *Expected result:* Builds trust and strengthens your profile reputation.
  - Check messages regularly and reply in a timely manner.
  - Politely decline any requests to communicate outside the platform.

- **Focus on project-related discussions**
  *Expected result:* Clear, professional, and relevant communication.
  - Discuss project scope, timelines, deliverables, and revisions only.
  - Avoid sharing unrelated personal opinions or information.

## FAQ

**What if a client asks for my phone number or email?**
Politely refuse and remind them that all communication must stay on TechSynergy.

**Can I attach files?**
Yes, but only relevant project files. Avoid sharing sensitive personal information.

**What happens if I share personal info?**
This may compromise your safety and violate platform rules. Report any issues to support.

**How quickly should I respond to messages?**
Within 24 hours is recommended for professionalism and client trust.

## Troubleshooting

- **Client insists on communicating outside TechSynergy**
  Fix: Refuse politely and report the request to support.

- **I received an unprofessional or offensive message**
  Fix: Use the reporting tool and block the sender if necessary.

- **I accidentally shared personal information**
  Fix: Notify support immediately and stop further communication off-platform.
