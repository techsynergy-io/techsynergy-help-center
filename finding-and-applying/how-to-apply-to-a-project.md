# How to Apply to a Project

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

### What you'll achieve

- Apply for projects quickly on TechSynergy
- Allow project owners to review your profile and skills
- Receive project invitations from clients who find your profile suitable

### Prerequisites

- A TechSynergy Freelancer account (verification or badges are **not required**)

### Steps to Apply to a Project

- **Find a Project**
  - Browse projects on your dashboard or use the search function.
- **Review Project Details**
  - Click on a project to see the description, requirements, and budget.
- **Click "Apply"**
  - Your application is submitted immediately. No Projects or extra information are required.

### What Happens Next

- The project owner will review your profile and skills.
- If your profile matches the project requirements, the project owner may **accept your application**.
- Project owners can also **invite you to apply to their projects proactively** if they find your profile suitable.
- Once accepted, you can communicate with the Business and start working on the project.

### Expected Result

- You are successfully applied to projects with a single click.
- Project owners can evaluate your skills and invite you to projects proactively.
- You have multiple opportunities to be selected for projects without extra effort.

### Troubleshooting

**I don't see the Apply button:**

- Make sure you are logged into your Freelancer account.

**I applied but haven't heard back:**

- Wait for the project owner to review your profile. You can also apply to other projects to increase your chances.

**I received a project invitation:**

- Check notifications or applied projects under My project to view and respond to invitations.
