# Browse projects (filters, recommendations, match score)

**Who this is for:** Freelancer

### What you'll achieve

- Master the search tools to find high-fit technical opportunities.
- Understand how the AI-driven "Match Score" helps prioritize your applications.

### Prerequisites

- A completed and verified Freelancer profile (for accurate matching).
- At least 3 verified skills in your Skill Matrix.

### Steps

- **Enter the Marketplace:** Navigate to the "Find Projects" tab from your dashboard.
- **Apply Filters:** Narrow your search by **Category** (e.g., Mobile Dev), **Budget Range**, **Project Duration**.
- **Save Your Search:** Click the "Save" icon to instantly save a project you would like to apply for.

### FAQ

- **How often are new projects posted?** In real-time; the marketplace updates in a mili second.
- **Can I filter by specific tech stacks (e.g., "Next.js")?** Yes, use the "Technical Stack" filter for granular searching.
- **What are "Recommended for You" projects?** These are manually curated by our AI based on your "spikes" in the Skill Spider Graph.

### Troubleshooting

- **No projects appearing:** Ensure your filters aren't too restrictive (e.g., a very high minimum budget).
- **Match Score seems inaccurate:** Check if you have added the relevant skills to your profile; the AI can only match what it can see.
- **Saved search alerts not arriving:** Verify your notification settings and check your junk folder.
